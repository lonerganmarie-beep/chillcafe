The Chill Cafe - Harcourt Street Dublin

The Chill Cafe Bar is Dublin's funkiest places located in the heart of Dublin. Our Cafe is famous for it's single sourced coffee blends and homemade cakes. Our staff love to be creative with the lunch time menu, all made fresh on our premises. Our bar opens at 5pm and you can enjoy music from Thursdays to Sundays. We brew our own beer on site called Chill beer.
